About Gemirasa

Gemirasa celebrates Sri Lanka's rich food traditions. From our home in Ratnapura, we make traditional Sri Lankan sweets like Mun Kewum, Kokis, Aluwa, and Asmi and many more using old family recipes.

These sweets are handmade by skilled local women who care about taste and quality. We use only natural ingredients and follow clean, safe methods to make sure every item is fresh, tasty, and healthy.

At Gemirasa, each sweet tells a story of love, culture, and tradition. We take pride in preserving these authentic recipes that have been passed down through generations, ensuring that every bite connects you to Sri Lanka's rich culinary heritage.
